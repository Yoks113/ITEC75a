CAVITE STATE UNIVERSITY - TC

MISSION

CvSU aims to provide excellent, equitable, and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences, and technology through quality instruction and responsive research and development activities. The university strives to produce professional, skilled, and morally upright individuals who are globally competitive.

VISION

CvSU envisions itself as the premier university in historic Cavite, globally recognized for excellence in character development, academics, research, innovation, and sustainable community engagement.
